# Privacy Policy for AeroGuard

**Last Updated: December 27, 2025**

## Introduction

AeroGuard ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Location Data
- **Purpose**: To provide you with accurate air quality information for your current location
- **Type**: Approximate location (city-level)
- **Usage**: Location data is used only to fetch relevant air quality data and is not stored on our servers
- **Control**: You can disable location access in your device settings at any time

### Automatically Collected Information
- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system version
- **Usage Data**: App features used, crash reports for improving app stability
- **Purpose**: To improve app performance and user experience

## Information We Do NOT Collect

- We do NOT collect personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
- We do NOT track your precise GPS location
- We do NOT sell your data to third parties
- We do NOT use your data for advertising purposes

## Third-Party Services

AeroGuard uses the following third-party services to provide functionality:

### OpenWeatherMap API
- **Purpose**: To fetch air quality and weather data
- **Data Shared**: Your approximate location (latitude/longitude)
- **Privacy Policy**: [https://openweathermap.org/privacy-policy](https://openweathermap.org/privacy-policy)

### Firebase (if applicable)
- **Purpose**: Analytics and crash reporting
- **Data Collected**: Anonymous usage statistics, crash logs
- **Privacy Policy**: [https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy](https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy)

## Data Storage and Security

- Location data is processed in real-time and not permanently stored
- Favorite locations are stored locally on your device
- We use industry-standard security measures to protect data transmission
- No user accounts or cloud storage of personal data

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access location services settings and disable them at any time
- Delete the app and all locally stored data
- Contact us with privacy concerns

## Children's Privacy

AeroGuard does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app is designed for general audiences and does not require age verification.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:
- Updating the "Last Updated" date
- Posting the new Privacy Policy in the app and on our website

## Data Retention

- Location data: Not stored (processed in real-time only)
- Favorite locations: Stored locally until you delete them or uninstall the app
- Analytics data: Retained for up to 14 months for app improvement purposes

## International Data Transfers

Air quality data is fetched from international servers. By using AeroGuard, you consent to the transfer of your location data to these services for the sole purpose of providing air quality information.
